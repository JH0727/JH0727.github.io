Relationship between inequality and economic growth

Aim of the project

The aim of this project is to examine the relationship between inequality and growth in an economic perspective.
The main questions that will be considered are:
1. Does the increase in income inequality hinders economic growth?
2. Does any other inequality hinders economic growth?
3. Does economic growth worsen the inequality?
4. Will the poorer economies tend to grow faster than richer economies?
